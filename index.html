About

Discover Alex & Carro, the enchanting husband-and-wife duo blending folk and pop in a captivating musical journey since December 2023. Alex, with a rich history in blues, rock, and solo performances featured on BBC Introducing and Sveriges Radio, brings a soulful edge with guitar parts that are easy on the ear. Carro, steeped in folk and classical music, adds a melodic depth with her instrumental prowess. Together, they craft a unique sound that's both familiar and fresh, resonating with audiences across genres. Their music isn't just a fusion of styles, but a harmonious blend of their lives and passions. Join Alex & Carro as they chart a new path in the folk-pop landscape, one song at a time
